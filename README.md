# Digital-Cemetery-Ancestry-Tracker
Problem Statement – Digital Cemetery CRM
Traditional cemetery management is still handled manually through paper-based records, registers, or fragmented systems. This leads to multiple issues such as:
Inefficient Plot Management: Difficulty in tracking available and occupied burial plots, leading to double-bookings or errors.
Poor Record-Keeping: Family, burial, and maintenance details are often scattered, incomplete, or hard to retrieve when required.
Approval Delays: Approvals for burial services and plot allocations are slow, especially during emergencies or holidays.
Limited Transparency: Families have no digital access to check availability, services, or status updates.
Operational Challenges: Managers struggle to generate real-time reports on usage, revenue, and maintenance activities.
👉 To address these challenges, the Digital Cemetery CRM will be built on Salesforce to:
Provide a centralized system for managing burial plots, records, and services.
Ensure error-free booking and approvals with automation.
Offer secure access to sensitive family information.
Generate reports and dashboards for managers to analyze cemetery usage, revenue, and operations.
Enable better customer service through quick information retrieval and case management.
